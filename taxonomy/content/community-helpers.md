# Community helpers content sheet v1 — images + teaching descriptions

For review. Generic (NOT the child) — shared by every kid, organized by where he meets them.
Descriptions: **who they are/what they do → what they wear/use → where you see them.**
`{style}` = art style token. No `{reference}` — these are other people, friendly and approachable.
