# Disclosure placement map + short-form UI copy (DRAFT for review)

Where each legal/consent touchpoint goes in the actual flow, with the exact short-form
copy to drop in. Long-form documents live at `/privacy` and `/terms` (drafts in this
folder). Each item lists the file to edit when approved.

---

## 1. Account creation — the master consent
**Where:** signup.html, accept-invite.html, and the native OnboardingFlow account step —
directly under the password field, blocking the Create button until checked.

> ☐ I'm the parent or legal guardian (or a caregiver with their permission), I'm 18+,
> and I agree to the [Terms of Service](/terms) and [Privacy Policy](/privacy).
> I understand photos I upload are used only to illustrate our board.

One checkbox, four assertions — this is the COPPA parental-consent anchor. Log the
timestamp + policy version with the account row (`users.consented_at`,
`users.consent_version`).

## 2. Child photo step (onboarding)
**Where:** onboard.html child_photo step + native flow, small text under the camera button.

> Your child's photo is used only to draw their portrait and personalized tiles in your
> family's art style. It's sent securely to our AI art providers for that one purpose,
> never used to train their models, and stored privately. You can delete it any time.

## 3. Parent / family photo steps (onboarding + Family & people + AddTileView people)
**Where:** onboard.html parent_photo/family steps, PersonEditorSheet, AddTileView people section.

> Only upload a photo of someone who's given you permission (you're vouching for that).
> It's used solely to draw their tile for your child's board.

## 4. Voice & audio
**Where:** voice-picker step (onboard.html) + wherever parents record cheer phrases
(parent.html celebrations panel).

> Tile voices are synthetic, generated by our speech partner. Recordings you make are
> stored privately and used only to play on your child's board.

## 5. Listening mode — the microphone disclosure
**Where:** First activation of listening mode, web + native (one-time confirm), plus the
MessageBoardView listening card.

> Listening mode turns speech near the tablet into tiles **on the screen, live**. Audio
> is processed for transcription and not kept as a recording. Everyone in the room
> should be okay with that — it stops automatically after 2 minutes of quiet.

(Also verify `NSMicrophoneUsageDescription` / `NSSpeechRecognitionUsageDescription` /
`NSCameraUsageDescription` / `NSPhotoLibraryUsageDescription` strings in Info.plist say
the same things in one sentence each.)

## 6. Store / subscription screen — Apple-required block
**Where:** StoreView.swift bottom + store.html footer. Apple Review 3.1.2 requires the
auto-renew disclosure and functional Terms + Privacy links **inside the app**.

> **My World Plus** is $9.99/month and adds 50 credits monthly. Payment is charged to
> your Apple ID at confirmation of purchase. The subscription renews automatically
> unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period; manage or
> cancel in Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. Credits are non-refundable once spent
> on completed images — but every image you make is yours to keep.
> [Terms of Service](/terms) · [Privacy Policy](/privacy)

## 7. Medical disclaimer
**Where:** Footer of parent.html + welcome/onboarding completion screen + App Store
description.

> My World supports communication practice at home. It isn't a medical device and
> doesn't replace evaluation or therapy from your child's care team.

## 8. AI-content note
**Where:** First-run of the Add-tiles flow (one-time), and the Word Shop footer.

> Tile art is AI-generated illustration — likenesses are approximate and pictures can
> be imperfect. Every image includes a free guided retry.

## 9. Account deletion
**Where:** EXISTS on web (parent.html). **MISSING in the iOS app — Apple requires
in-app account deletion for apps with in-app account creation (Guideline 5.1.1(v)).**
Add a "Delete account…" row to ParentSettingsView → Account section that calls
`POST /api/auth/delete-account` after typing DELETE, mirroring the web flow.

## 10. Footer links everywhere
**Where:** index.html, welcome.html, signup.html, login.html, store.html, parent.html,
onboard.html; native: Settings screen + StoreView.

> [Terms](/terms) · [Privacy](/privacy) · [Support]([SUPPORT EMAIL])
