## Recommended changes (priority order)

1. **Verbs → "Actions".** Your *Fletcher Asks for Things* + *Fletcher Does Things* are both the official **Actions** category. Rename/merge (keep them as two sub-groups under Actions if you like).
2. **"Living Things" → split into Animals + Nature.** Rename **Living Things → Animals**, and move its **Plants** sub-group out to a new **Nature** category (official keeps animals in Animals, plants in Nature).
3. **Pull "Body parts" out of Learning.** Official **Body** is its own top-level category. Move *Learning / Body parts → Body*. (Your *Learning / Weather* officially lives under **Describing** — optional.)
4. **Utensils aren't food.** Knife, Fork, Spoon, Plate, Bowl, Scissors, Scoop belong in **Home / Mealtime**, not Food.
5. **Create the missing language groups.** The board has no **Core strip**, **Pronouns** (I/you/we/he/she/they), **Linking** (is/can/the/and), **Asking** (what/where/who), **Quantifiers**, or **Adverbs**. These are high-value AAC vocabulary — worth building.
6. **Core-word placement is a real choice.** You put core words (*more, stop, go, eat, open, look, again, wait…*) in the **Verbs** column; the official model keeps them in the persistent **Core strip**. For strict parity, move them to the strip — but if the Verbs placement works for Fletcher, keep it and we note the divergence.

**Leave personal (no parity expected):** Movies, Fletcher Talks, Fletcher Tries New Food, Speech Therapy (toys/places/therapists), the family's real names, Mother's Day phrases, the "X is for Y" letter tiles.
