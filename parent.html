Teach Me Slideshow

Each picture shows with its word — spoken, then every teaching clue read aloud. Same show as the board's 📖 button, but you pick the words.

Quiz · Active

Clue Quiz

Hears a teaching clue ("you drink it when you're thirsty") and taps the picture it describes. Each miss reveals another clue; a correct tap plays the word.

👂
Mode 5 · Active

Auditory Comprehension

Hear a description ("lives in a field, four legs, eats grass") and pick the matching picture. Builds receptive comprehension and inference, not just word-to-image recognition.

🗣️
Mode 6 · Active

Expressive Naming

An image alone — no audio prompt. Fletcher names it unaided (voice / gesture / object). You mark the response from your phone. This is the ideal: unprompted expressive production.

🎉
Mode 7 · Reward

Celebration

Yellow flowers burst and your recorded voice cheers Fletcher on. Plays at session end.
