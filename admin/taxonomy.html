Import rows

Accepts CSV, TSV, or XLSX. Required columns: id, column, label, subject_mode, parent_photo_behavior, prompt_template. Optional: category, subcategory, pronunciation, phase, core (true/false, default true), status, notes, growth_stage, meal_context, is_gestalt, gestalt_type, gestalt_meaning, gestalt_target_words (comma-separated), descriptive_clues (pipe- or newline-separated). Aliases: name→label, mode→subject_mode, behavior→parent_photo_behavior, prompt→prompt_template.

Backfill taxonomy slugs

Links a child's existing tiles to canonical taxonomy entries by case-insensitive label match within the same section. Matched items get their taxonomy_slug populated, and historical game_attempts for matched items also receive the slug so per-concept mastery starts rolling up against past sessions immediately. Ambiguous (multiple candidates) and unmatched items are skipped — you can re-run after editing labels.

Snapshots

Every import auto-creates a snapshot. Snapshots are immutable; restoring one auto-creates a pre-restore snapshot first.
